About Us

YLG Empowers Individuals & Business Persons to Migrate to Canada

YLG - Yazdani Law Group is a leading Toronto law firm practicing primarily in the area of immigration, business and Property law for Canada. This is accomplished through a Canadian legal service experience, which simplifies complexity and is supported and sustained by long-term relationships.

Immigration Law

If you are considering immigration to Canada, YLG will give you all the knowledge and tools you need to make an informed decision and build a successful life in Canada.

Business Law

YLG gives you the opportunity to receive first-hand exposure to the latest developments in Canadian immigration laws and policies, business context and opportunities.

Property Law

YLG covers all aspects of condominium development and management, as well as litigation matters. YLG acts on behalf of developers and individual unit owners.
